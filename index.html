About

Foodies, you've found the ultimate corner for all of your favorite, decadent recipes. Whether you love to cook, thoroughly enjoy eating or simply collect recipes, this is for you. Interested in learning how to cook? Browse meals that cover the basics of baking and cooking, as well as those geared toward the more adventurous and experienced cooks. Learn how to achieve gastronomic success with gourmet recipes, and pick up a few new skills along the way from authors who share your enthusiasm for cooking. If you have a garden full of garlic or onions this year, look up a specialty titles, and you'll be on your way to using up all of those fresh vegetables.
From busy days to holiday seasons, this recipe book is filled with delectable delights for any palate.
